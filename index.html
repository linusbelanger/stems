WHAT IS MUSIC PRODUCTION?

Producing music is the process of bringing a song idea to fruition. It involves writing, composing, recording, arranging, editing, mixing, and mastering. A music producer uses a software called a DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) like Logic Pro or Pro Tools, along with a number of instruments and plugins, real and virtual, to go from a voice memos demo to a polished, distribution-ready track.


AND STEMS?

STEMS aims to help make music production just a little more accessible, providing creators with important knowledge and powerful tools at no barrier of entry.


HOW DO I USE IT?

STEMS operates as a web-based archive of free music production resources. The site is arranged like a DAW to help beginners become acquainted with the environment. All the recorded material exists in the large grid on the right, the plugins in the panel on the left, and mixing/mastering at the bottom. Simply click any of the sections to learn more and access the tools.
