TRACKS:

Instru­ments

Instrument tracks include everything from guitar and bass to piano and synth. These tracks make up the main musical body of the song, providing things like chords, root notes, melodies, and solos. They can be real recorded instruments or virtual VST instruments.

Free VST instrument include Spitfire LABS, Vital Wavetable Synth, Addictive Keys, DecentSampler, and many more.
