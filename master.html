TRACKS:

MIX &
MAS­TER

Mixing is the process of making sure every track in your song works together smoothly and intentionally. Ensuring the overall mix doesn't exceed 0dB (or "clip") is called gain staging and is an important final step of mixing a song before mastering.

Mastering is the process of pollishing the final song for distribution. It involves overall EQ, stereo imaging, compression, and, maybe most importantly, limiting. A limiter maximizes volume without unwanted clipping.
